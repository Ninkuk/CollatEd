Meet The Team

Get to know us!

Sara Ketabi is a part of the UNICEF USA National Council of Leadership Development. She spearheads social entrepreneurship, global health, and global citizenship for sustainable development to improve the current global education system. She is Founder and CEO of Hale, a biotechnology startup that works towards engineering solutions to eradicating poverty. Sara has spoken at the UNICEF Global Annual Summit on leading through the Sustainable Development Goals , UN HLPF, and will be speaking the Women's Economic Forum and Ministerial UN SDG Summit in these upcoming months. Sara's favorite part of CollatEd is upholding its values of systemic change in the ways students fundamentally approach education.

Anrui Gu is a student at UC Berkeley and the original ideator for the Solve Young, Solve Strong movement. An international student from China, she has received highest cultural ambassadorship honors in her home country, and has learned the importance of information decentralization through multiple editorial roles, ranging from grassroots Chinatown to cutting-edge blogs. She plans to take CollatEd to another level by balancing the coordination of events and software to achieve the right amount of personalization, and grow with CollatEd in the facility of understanding and countering area-specific poverty.

Welcome to the era of Massive Open Online Discovery (MOOD)! CollatEd is a worldwide education reform movement for Emerging Student Innovators, including the enforcement of UN Education Sustainable Development goals, the promotion of open innovation, and the virtual connection of the knowledge systems in society.
