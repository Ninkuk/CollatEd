2020-2021 Global Steering Committee

In an ever-globalizing world, members of the Global Steering Committee are at the forefront of both inspiring and actualizing effective change. Coming from diverse backgrounds, transcending geographic boundaries, and operating under different governmental structures, the factor that binds all members is the willingness to apply proven knowledge to effect societal development.
